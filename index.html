About me

My name is chicken, identified as omnivores, which refers to an specie that can survice by consuming both plant and animal matter. I have wings, but I am not capable of long-distance flight. You may only be able to see me fly through a few fences, or jump up to a window.

Social Behavior

Flocks

We live in flocks, having an leader to dominate the whole family. If you remove the leader from this flock, the remaining chicken or young birds would fall into disruption or disorder.

More details

Puff up feathers

When we ran into a predator, stressed or in sick. We will puff up our feathers.

More details

Cockfight

In the 16 century, people would intentionally breed us to have stronger stamina and strength. The bloodsport between chickens will be held on a ring, and we were called gamefowl at that era. People would bet at one of us, so it is a sort of gambling plus entertainment at that period.

Origin

We belongs to the order of Galliformes, and are descended primarily from the red junglefowl. Our origin is from Southeast Asia, which red junglefowl is originated from India.
